Safe AI Germany Co-Director · Research Incubator Lead

Scaling AI safety research and community across Germany

SAIGE runs a national research incubator pairing early-career researchers with mentors on concrete AI safety projects. I design the selection process, build evaluation infrastructure, and coordinate mentorship across institutions.

Research

My research focuses on LLM safety evaluation — making evaluations context-aware, multilingual, and grounded in real user welfare rather than abstract benchmarks.

Featured

Challenges of Evaluating LLM Safety for User Welfare

Context-aware evaluation reveals safety gaps of ~1.8–2.5 points for high-vulnerability users in finance and health domains. Prompt enrichment alone doesn't close these gaps.
